About ICICCT 2026

The Fourth International Conference on Innovative Computing and Cutting-edge Technologies (ICICCT 2026) brings together experts and participants from academia and industry to exchange ideas and present results of ongoing research in state-of-the-art areas in computing and information management.

Organized by the Middle East Association of Computer Science and Engineering (MEACSE), ICICCT 2026 provides a comprehensive global forum for researchers and practitioners to collaborate, innovate, and shape the future of computing technology.

Whether you are a researcher, academic, or industry professional, ICICCT 2026 offers the perfect platform to showcase your work and connect with peers from around the world.
