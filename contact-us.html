Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a paper?

Papers can be submitted through our Submission page. You'll need to create an account, provide paper details, and upload your PDF file. The deadline is May 31, 2026.

What is the registration deadline?

Early bird registration closes on September 1, 2026. Standard registration continues until the conference begins. Visit our Registration page for more details.

Do I need a visa to visit Turkey?

Visa requirements depend on your nationality. Many citizens don't require a visa for Turkey. We recommend checking with your embassy or our About Turkey page for current requirements.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, and wire transfers. Payment details will be provided after registration.

Can I get a refund if I need to cancel?

Cancellations made before September 1, 2026 are eligible for a full refund. After that date, a 50% refund is provided. No refunds are available within 7 days of the conference.

Is there accommodation available for attendees?

Yes, Istanbul has many accommodation options from luxury hotels to budget-friendly options. We recommend booking early as September is peak season. Check our About Turkey page for recommendations.
